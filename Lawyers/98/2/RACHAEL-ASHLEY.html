RACHAEL ASHLEY

Associate

Dubai

+971 4 429 2999

r.ashley@opconsults.com
  download vcard

Rachael is an Associate in the commercial team in Dubai and regularly advises clients on a range of commercial matters.

Her experience includes drafting, negotiating and advising on supply and consultancy agreements, agency, franchising, distribution, and commercial due diligence in relation to a variety of business transactions.

Rachael advises a broad spectrum of clients across different sectors and jurisdictions on matters such as international franchising and distribution, intellectual property, outsourcing, consumer protection, consultancy, ICT contracts (including software licensing), TMT related agreements, e-commerce and competition.

Rachael has also completed a nine month secondment to a UK bank and a secondment to a UK University’s research and development team.

Prior to joining Ommar Platinum Consults, Rachael practised at international law firm, Eversheds, headquartered in London. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in law from University College London and is admitted as a Solicitor of the Law Society of England and Wales.
