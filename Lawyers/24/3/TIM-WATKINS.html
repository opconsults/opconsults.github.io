TIM WATKINS

Partner

Dubai

+971 4 4292999

t.watkins@opconsults.com
  download vcard

Tim is a Partner in Ommar Platinum Consults Corporate department in Dubai. He has an extensive range of corporate and commercial experience including cross border acquisitions including related experience in real estate, employment and projects.
 
Having been in the UAE for 15 years, Tim has an expert knowledge of the region developed through working with a number of leading multi-national organizations. He is known for his strong understanding of both the legal framework and the commercial realities within which companies in the region must operate. Tim has consequently worked on some of the UAE’s largest high profile projects, such as the establishment of Abu Dhabi’s national airline, Etihad Airways.
 
Tim studied Law at the University of Birmingham (UK) and went on to work for a number of International law firms in both the UK and the UAE, including Trowers & Hamlins LLP, Reed Smith and DLA Piper LLP.
