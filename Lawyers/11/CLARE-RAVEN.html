CLARE RAVEN

Partner

Dubai

+971 4 4292999

c.raven@opconsults.com
  download vcard

Clare is a general commercial litigator with experience in resolving all types of commercial and contractual disputes. She has successfully represented clients in cases spanning multiple jurisdictions.
 
Clare has experience of international arbitrations (including ICC, DIAC and LCIA), mediations, English High Court and DIFC Court proceedings where she has full rights of audience to appear on behalf of clients.
 
Acting on claims across a number of industries including banking and financial services (including regulatory matters), aviation, pharmaceuticals, professional services, construction, property and civil engineering, Clare has represented a range of clients including banks, major airlines, blue chip companies, property developers and international accountancy firms.
 
Clare was admitted as a solicitor in England and Wales in 2002 and prior to joining Ommar Platinum Consults was an associate with Stephenson Harwood, London. Outside of the office Clare is a qualified skipper and enjoys sailing whenever she can.

  • THE IBA GUIDELINES ON PARTY REPRESENTATION IN INTERNATIONAL ARBITRATION 2013

    The International Bar Association (IBA) is a worldwide association of legal practitioners, with a membership of over 50,000 individual lawyers and over 200 law societies and bar associations. The IBA seeks to “influence the development of international law reform throughout the world.” In May 2013, the Arbitration Committee published Guidelines on Party Representation in International Arbitration. The Guidelines address issues of counsel conduct and are intended to provide some standardisation of conduct by legal representatives. The Guidelines may be adopted by the parties and/or the Tribunal in an arbitration.

  • MEDIATION IN THE UAE

    There are a number of formal types of mediation in the UAE and sometimes disputing parties adopt informal mediations in order to try to resolve their disputes. In this article Clare Raven of Ommar Platinum Consults will consider the more frequently used types of mediation deployed in the UAE with a particular focus on Dubai. This is not intended to be an exhaustive discussion of all types of mediation.
