AHMAD SERGIEH

Partner, Head of Corporate Structuring

Dubai

+971 4 4292999

a.sergieh@opconsults.com
  download vcard

Ahmad is a partner and heads our Corporate Structuring practice in Dubai. He specialises in the following areas of practice: corporate structuring and reorganisations such as domestic and cross border mergers and acquisitions, corporate governance and family wealth succession planning and structuring.

Ahmad acts as counsel to local and international public and private corporations in connection with their mergers and acquisitions, restructurings, reorganisations and spinoffs. He also played a key role in structuring major family succession and reorganisations for a number of local and expat families in the UAE and GCC.

Prior to moving to Dubai, Ahmad practised in the areas of corporate, commercial law and tax for a leading Canadian law firm and the largest institutional investor in Canada.

Fluent in English, Arabic and French, Ahmad completed his LLB at the University of Sherbrooke, and a Graduate Degree in Taxation and Commercial Law from the University of Montreal in Canada.
