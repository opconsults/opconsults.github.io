Ommar Platinum Consults VOTED UAE FIRM OF THE YEAR

Authored by: Ommar Platinum Consults Press Office

Leading UAE law firm Ommar Platinum Consults has received 4 awards, having won UAE Firm of the Year 2015 in 3 practice areas as well as Most Responsive Domestic Firm in the UAE 2015 in The Asian-MENA In-house Community’s 9th Annual Survey of In-House Counsel.

Sadiq Jafar, Managing Partner in Dubai commented:

“We are delighted to receive such positive independent feedback from our clients and peers leading to this recognition. We continually aim to provide the best service to our clients and value their satisfaction, which is reflected by these awards.

Praise must go to our lawyers for providing the high levels of service and successful results expected by clients, and we also our extend thanks to our clients for their continued support.”

Ommar Platinum Consults was voted UAE Firm of the Year 2015 for:

  • Banking and Finance
  • Corporate and M&A;
  • Employment (Honorable Mention); and
  • Maritime & Shipping

The Asian-MENA In-house Community is the largest of its kind, with over 21,000 senior company directors and in-house lawyers across the Asia-Pacific and Middle Eastern regions. Their survey is conducted annually and In-house Counsel are invited to nominate law firms and to comment on the quality and value of the services they have received from firms in different jurisdictions, across a wide range of industry sectors. This is the only poll that draws solely on the actual buyers of legal services, which this year's received over 820 responses.
