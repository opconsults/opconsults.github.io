Ommar Platinum Consults wins two of the The Oath Magazine's 2016 Team of the Year Awards

Authored by: Ommar Platinum Consults Press Office

Ommar Platinum Consults was awarded both the Corporate Team and Real Estate & Construction Team of the Year at The Middle East Legal Awards 2016 hosted by The Oath Magazine. The awards ceremony was held on 22 November 2016 and honours the exceptional accomplishments of the region’s legal profession.
